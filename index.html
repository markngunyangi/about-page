Why I Started This Site

What's wrong with the current system ?

Most people have not learned how much the world would be better with a little bit of generosity and kindness. Everybody is trying to take a piece of the cake for themselves. Writing companies have hired expert designers and content creators, and they know what to write, to get you enticed so that they have your business. As a result, they position themselves, with numbers and testimonials, as companies that can do a great job for you, and they charge you for it, and then, it ends there. How you afford the high prices is all up to you. No writing company is asking themselves, “With such high prices, how will these students manage to pay for a whole semester’s number of assignments?” Also, there is somewhat, a kind of a trap with such services, because, once they start writing your paper professionally, you have to keep presenting such quality of papers the whole semester, otherwise, you risk getting caught for academic cheating; how frustrating. Wouldn’t you honestly like a change? Don’t you look forward to someone guiding you through the process?

What distinguishes me from the rest ?

Having been a writer for almost 8 years now, I don’t accuse these writing companies so much, because I was just like them; trying to get more work, more clients and more money. I didn’t quite care about how my clients would afford the prices. The human side of me has now taken me back, and I need to be a contributor, not a diminisher. When it comes to writing essays, I know what the professor looks for, and I know how to get it well-structured in a paper, for my clients. I have written a whole guide for that.
